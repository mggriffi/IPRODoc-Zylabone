Create Review Batch

Review batches are workloads for reviewers. They are created based on the (selected) documents in the Document List (result set).

Tip: Sort the Document List. When splitting the batch into sub-batches, this sorting order is used.

  1. Define a Name for the Batch.

  2. Select a method to split your batch into sub-batches:

    • Number of sub-batches.
      For example, 8 sub-batches for 8 reviewers.

    • Number of documents per sub-batch.
      For example, a maximum of 500 documents per reviewer.

  3. Define how email messages should be handled:

    • Keep email conversations in one batch.
      Items with hits and belonging to one conversation are combined in one of the sub-batches.

    • Include complete email conversations.
      Items with and without hits (not part of the Document List) and belonging to one conversation are combined in one of the sub-batches.

  4. Define how items from a family should be handled: 

    • Keep email conversations in one batch.
      Items with hits and belonging to one conversation are combined in one of the sub-batches.

    • Include complete email conversations.
      Items with and without hits (not part of the Document List) and belonging to one conversation are combined in one of the sub-batches.

  5. Define how duplicates should be handled:

    • Include Duplicates.

    • Exclude Duplicate Documents.

    • Exclude Duplicate Families.

  6. Click Create.
