Matter Management

View, filter, sort, create, edit, delete or export matters.

  • View created matters and filter/sort them via the columns.

  • Via the Settings icon  add/remove columns or export the Matter Management View as CSV, Excel or PDF.

  • Edit a matter via the Edit Matter icon . 

  • Delete a single matter via the Delete icon .

  • Delete multiple matters by selecting them and clicking the Delete selected Matters icon .

  • Update the Matter Management View with the Refresh icon .

Create Matter

  1. Select Create Matter.

  2. Define the Matter Details.

  3. Click Save.
