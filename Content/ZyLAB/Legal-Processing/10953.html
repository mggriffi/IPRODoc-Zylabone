Transfer a Matter

Conditions

You want to transfer a checked-out Matter to another user. This user should have the Modify Matter permission for that specific matter.

Depending on the permissions you have received, you may not be able to use the Transfer Lock option. This permission is usually granted to the Global Administrator role.

Instructions

  1. ZyLAB Legal Processing is open.
  2. In the Matter tab, select a Matter.
  3. In the Matter Card, select Transfer Lock.

  4. Select a new user.
  5. Click the Transfer button.
  6. Click Yes.
    The Lock (checked-out state of the Matter) will be transferred to the new user. The original user will not be able to check in. He will get a message that the Matter is not checked out by him.
