Collect Data from Multiple Legal Processing Matters

It is now possible to collect data from multiple Legal Processing matters one by one to one existing Legal Review matter. Only one Legal Processing Matter should be active at a time and to create a new Legal Processing Matter (to collect from to a Legal Review Matter), the previous Legal Processing Matter should be deleted, not the Legal Review matter part.

Instructions

  1. Delete (not just close) old Legal Processing Matter that was collecting to a Legal Review Matter.
  2. Add <ExistingLegalReviewMatterId>existingLrMatterId</ExistingLegalReviewMatterId> to LegalReviewTemplate in EdiscoveryService.
  3. Define a new Legal Processing Matter and start collecting to the same Legal Review Matter.
  4. If no next Legal Processing Matter has to be added, delete <ExistingLegalReviewMatterId>existingLrMatterId</ExistingLegalReviewMatterId> from the LegalReviewTemplate, to enable normal Legal Processing again.
