Delete a Matter

Conditions

You want to delete a Matter.

Important Notice: Cleanup/delete matters during off-peak hours, since the process could take a long time.

Instructions

  1. ZyLAB Legal Processing is open.
  2. In the Matters tab, select a Matter.
  3. Click Close Matter.

  4. Click Close Matter.

Result

You have deleted a Matter.
