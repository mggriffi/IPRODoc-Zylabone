View or Stop Job

 

View or Stop Job

A job is activated by the Job Schedule which is defined for each matter. For more information, see Define the Job Schedule.

If a matter is being run, you can view or stop it via the Jobs tab.

If a job is double-clicked or when you click the Go To Actions button, you will be directed to the Actions tab.



The Actions tab displays all the actions for the matter to which the job belongs.

View Job

  • Sort jobs via Arrange By and select from:


    • Nothing
      Jobs will not be sorted.
    • Matter
      Jobs will be sorted based on Matter. The user is able to collapse/expand the Actions tree of a matter with the arrow icon.
    • Status
      Jobs will be sorted based on their status: In Progress, Completed, Stopped By User.
    • Start Date
      Jobs will be sorted based on their start date, newest first.
  • If something has failed, a red text will be displayed.

Stop Job

Click the Cancel button of a job to stop it. You will be warned that the execution will be terminated.
