Document Preview

What

Preview documents quickly by hovering your cursor over document icon.

Why

To make it easier and quicker to locate the documents you need.

How

Hover your cursor over the document's icon in the grid to display the first page of the document.

Copy/Paste Matter Roles

What

Copy the configuration of one or more roles to paste into additional matters.

Why

To save time when configuring roles for multiple matters.

How

Select the Copy Role button associated with a single role, or Copy Roles to select all roles, locate the needed matter and click Paste Roles.

Delete Entity Type

What

Delete all entity results from Entity Management Page by entity type.

Why

Reduce unwanted noisy data and simplify entity results to what you need.

How
