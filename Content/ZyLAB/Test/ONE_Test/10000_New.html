Delete Document(s) and Remove Related Entities

What

When deleting single, multiple, or all document(s) from the matter, related entities are removed in Insights.

Why

Ensures the appropriate deletion and removal of document(s) and corresponding entities.

How

Delete by manual selection: in the Document List, select document(s). Then, open Settings, and click Delete.

Delete by query. Then, open Settings, select a List Option, and click Delete.

Permission to delete all documents selected for deletion, is required.
