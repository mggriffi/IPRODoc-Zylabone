Archive

An archive can be compared with a matter in ZyLAB ONE (Legal Review) or an index in the ZyLAB Information Management Platform (ZyINDEX).

An archive is often a collection of related documents, like a case or a project.


An archive contains files.

A file contains records (registrations).

A record contains binaries (documents).

 

Archives are created, edited and deleted via Settings > Archives.
