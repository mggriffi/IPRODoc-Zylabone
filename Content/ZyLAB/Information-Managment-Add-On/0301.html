ZyLAB ONE Information Management Add-on

Archive electronic and/or paper documents with the ZyLAB ONE Information Management Add-on and add metadata. Manage all your documents and metadata fast and easy from one single location. View and edit documents in ZyLAB ONE Legal Review. 

Note: This manual is also available in Dutch.

Key Features

  • Archive paper documents (for example, signed contracts).
    First, register the paper document in the Add-on. Then, print and scan (with ZySCAN) the registration document (with barcode and/or Dymo label) on top of the original paper document. Paper documents are converted to electronic documents using OCR (Optical Character Recognition). After that the document is searchable.
    The registration document serves two purposes: 
    • First, splitting documents when scanning multiple paper documents.
      This makes it possible to scan a pile of documents at once, after which each document is archived separately. This speeds up the process. 
    • Second, adding metadata to each document, like a unique registration number and other information.
      During/after scanning, the information from the registration document is linked to the paper document. 
  • Archive electronic documents from their original application to the Add-on.
    For example, in Microsoft Outlook, select the option 'Archive email to ZyLAB'.
  • Archive electronic documents from their location (for example a computer) to the Add-on.
    In the ZyLAB ONE Information Management Add-on, select the documents you want to upload/archive.
  • Add metadata to all archived documents.
    Metadata can be added before (to the registration document before scanning the paper documents), during or after archiving.
    Which metadata is added will be defined in the record type.
  • Archive multiple documents at once (in batch) with the same metadata.
  • Save archived documents on premise (index in ZyINDEX) or in the cloud (matter in ZyLAB ONE).        
  • Edit metadata directly in the Add-on or when working on a document in ZyINDEX/ZyLAB ONE Legal Review. All changes are automatically synchronized with the metadata in the ZyLAB ONE Information Management Add-on. After a correct synchronization, an  appears in the Status column of a record/binary.
