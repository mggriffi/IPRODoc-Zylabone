PRR & FOIA

Welcome

The Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) and similar open records acts allow the public to access records under the control of governmental agencies. These access rights state that public information shall be delivered within a strict time frame.

Modern eDiscovery platforms can be used to meet the specific requirements of the various public records acts. Agencies can then proactively prepare for timely responses to requests and predict the resources required.

Start with our videos and follow up with our use case, which guide you through the process of responding to open records requests with ZyLAB ONE.
