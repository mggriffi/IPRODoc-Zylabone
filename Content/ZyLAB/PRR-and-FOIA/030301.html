PRR & FOIA

Public records requests, either through the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) or other Open Records or Public Records Acts (PRA), has burdened agencies at all levels of the government with the need to conduct electronic discovery for records being requested by individuals and private parties. As records requests have dramatically increased, agencies have struggled to keep pace and some agencies now face request backlogs in the tens of thousands.

eDiscovery platforms such as ZyLAB ONE can be used to meet the specific requirements of the various public records acts so agencies can proactively prepare for timely responses to requests and make resource needs predictable.

This use case will guide you through the process for using ZyLAB ONE to respond to open records requests more efficiently.

ZyLAB ONE allows a single person to perform the work of many. The platform offers an organized, central location where your data is standardized, and all your public record response processes begin and end. With ZyLAB ONE you can quickly collect, review, redact and disclose your public records in a very efficient manner.
