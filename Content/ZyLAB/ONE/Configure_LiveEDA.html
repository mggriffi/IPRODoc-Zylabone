  3. Select from Available Custodians, using the arrows to move to Selected Custodians. They can be selected individually, or click Select All to move all custodians.

  4. Once Custodians are selected, click .

    Note: If you need to modify the Custodian list, move Custodians back to the Available Custodians column, and click the Apply Button.
