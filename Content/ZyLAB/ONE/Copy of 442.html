Reviewer Remarks

Remarks (max. 4000 characters by default) are saved when  icon is shown. Remarks are shown immediately in Properties widget (Remarks field).

  1. To add a remark, click in the Reviewer Remarks box.

  2. Insert your remark. 

    • When your remark is saved, the cloud icon appears.

    • The remark is stored in the field Remarks. You can find it in the Properties panel on the left side of the screen.
