Matter Management

Click to open Matter Management. This topic addresses various tasks you can complete while working with matters.

Add Matters

  1. Select  .

  2. Define the Matter Details. Only the Name is mandatory. Click Show More... to define more details.

  3. Click Save. 

Delete Matters

  • Delete a single matter via the Delete Matter  icon  (located at the end of the row).

  • Delete multiple matters by selecting them and clicking the Delete selected Matters icon (located at the top of the page).

View, Filter & Sort

View matters and filter/sort them via the columns. 

Note: Filtering/sorting works the same on all pages. View a short animation of filtering and sorting on this page: Hold Management.

  1. To add/remove columns, select the Settings icon .

  2. Then, select Columns and (de)select the appropriate columns.

  3. Update the Matter Management View with the Refresh icon  .

Work with Matters

Matter Management Options

  1. Double click the matter, or click to open the matter management options.

    This is where you can:

    • Edit the matter details.

    • View custodian holds and activity.

    • Work with holds.

    • Apply security to the matter.

    Overview

    The overview tab provides a matter summary.

    • Click Edit to open the Matter Details window for adding or deleting items.

    Custodians

    The Custodians tab is an overview of all custodians related to the matter. You can quickly view the custodian status and holds applied to each custodian.

    1. Click to view the Custodian Holds.

    2. Click to view the Custodian Activity. This will take you to the Audit screen.

      • Selecting a record will bring up the event details.

    Holds

    The Holds tab is an overview of the available holds that are related to the matter.

    1. Click to open the Legal Holds details page and create a new Hold.

    2. Click to open the Holds management page.

    3. Click to delete the Hold.

    Security

    The Security tab gives an overview of the security access for all users in the matter.

    1. To edit matter security, click to open the Matter Security window.

    Note: Global Hold Managers and Global Hold Viewers will always have access to all matters and holds.

Add a Label to a Matter

Labels help to organize matters, holds and custodians. You can quickly search and filter using labels.

  1. Click the Label icon.

  2. Enter the label name and click Save.

  3. The label icon will turn blue to indicate a label has been attached to that matter.

  4. Add Labels to your column view to quickly filter on the labels.

    • Click Settings>Columns>Check the box next to Labels.

    • Click Filter on Labels and select your label.

  5. Click Clear Filters to return to the main screen.

  6. Click Settings>Save View to keep Labels in your column view.

Add Labels to Multiple Matters

  1. Select your matters.

  2. Click the Label icon at the top of the screen.

  3. The Labels window will open indicating the labels in use. Add a new label and click Save. The new label is shown in blue and added to all items selected.

  4. Clicking on a label already created will add it to all items selected. Labels can be added and deleted on multiple matters using this method.

Export Matter Management View

  1. Export the Matter View in a CSV, Excel or PDF file.

  2. First, select the Settings icon  .

  3. Then, select Export As... and select the format to which you want to export.

Save View

The Matter Management View might have been adjusted by filtering, sorting and/or by a specific selection of columns (via Settings > Columns).

  1. To save this new view, select the Settings icon   .

  2. Then, select Save View.
