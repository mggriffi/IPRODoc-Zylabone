Hold Management

View, filter, sort, create, edit, delete or export Holds.

  • View created holds and sort/filter them via the columns.

  • Via the Settings icon  add/remove columns or export the Hold Management View as CSV, Excel or PDF.

  • Edit a hold via the Go to Hold icon .

  • Delete a single hold via the Delete icon  .

  • Delete multiple holds by selecting them and clicking the Delete selected Holds icon .

    Note: You can only delete a hold when it has been lifted.

  • Update the Holds Management View with the Refresh icon .
