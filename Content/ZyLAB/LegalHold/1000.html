Latest Features

As of version 65741 (January, 2025)

Hold Notifications

What

Improved sending notifications like reminders and escalations based on the custodian issue date and changed the Hold scheduling UI.

Why

To make the scheduling more transparent and make it easier to add custodians to an issued hold.

How

Notifications are now based on the custodian issue date/ time, and within the hold schedule settings, you can view per custodian when notifications will be sent.

As of version 63446 (July, 2024)

Escalation Notification

What

Changed the Escalation notification schedule to support escalations for custodians that are added later on.

Why

To make sure all custodians that are added after the Hold was issued, will move to the escalation workflow automatically if no responses are received.

How

Escalations are now based on Custodian issue date/time and no longer on Hold issue date/time.

Pasting Lists in Grid and Report Filters

What

  • You can paste a list of email addresses in the column filter option to quickly add custodians to a hold. No need to select these individually. Add Email Address Aliases

What

Add email address aliases to contacts in the Address Book.

Why

Include alternative email addresses and email aliases belonging to contacts. Find contacts by searching for their alternative addresses or aliases.

How

Complete Contact Synchronization configuration with Microsoft Entra ID or Okta. Navigate to the Admin Center > Subscription > Address Book Synchronization. Open Settings. Under Email Address Aliases, select the source (Microsoft Entra ID or Okta). Click Run Synchronization. Contacts' email address aliases can then be viewed from the Address Book.

Perform manual changes. Open the Address Book and create a new contact or edit an existing contact. Enter their alternative email addresses in the Email Address Alias field.

Contact Synchronizations and changes made to contacts, are recorded in the Tenants Audit.
