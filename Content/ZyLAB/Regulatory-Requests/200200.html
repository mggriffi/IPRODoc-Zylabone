Make All Data Searchable

Full automation requires all documents and information to be searchable. Certain parties with malicious intent will abuse automated processes by encrypting data, including non-searchable PDFs or using data containers such as zip-files, embedded data, multimedia (audio) or non-common languages. It is imperative to conduct deep processing in order to make everything searchable. For example, throughout the atomization you are able to categorize translations, coping, and spreadsheets become searchable.
