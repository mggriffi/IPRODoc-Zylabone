Delete a Web Client

Conditions

ZyINDEX > Web Client is selected.

Instructions

  1. Select the Web Client you want to delete.
  2. Click the Delete button: .
  3. If you want to delete the client, click Yes.

Result

The Web Client and its settings from your server's Registry are removed.

The physical files of the Web Client are removed.

Note

You cannot re-activate the client once you have deleted it.
