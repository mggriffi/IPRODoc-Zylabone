Matter Home Page

After you log in to ZyLAB ONE and select a matter on the Select Matter screen, the Matter Home page appears.

The features that appear on the Matter Home page are dependent on your user permissions, so you may not see all of the features listed on this page.

  1. Continue Review – returns you to the same location when you last logged out.
  2. Browse – opens the ZyLAB Facet dashboard or Document List view.
    Start here to begin your review of the collected documents.
  3. Batch Search – allows you to run bulk searches of key words and phrases.
  4. Production Overview – provide access to the previously produced documents.
  5. Configuration – ZyLAB ONE’s advanced configuration features.
  6. Upload – ZyLAB ONE’s document upload. Start here to ingest documents into your matter for review.
  7. Audit Log – view and report on auditable actions in your matter.
