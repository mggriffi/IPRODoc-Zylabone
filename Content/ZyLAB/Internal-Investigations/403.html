Insights

ZyLAB ONE offers a functionality that extracts PII (Personal Identifiable Information) from your data: Insights. This option can significantly speed up your investigation. By enabling Insights when you upload your data, you are able to extract every name, organization, location or other information that is useful for your investigation. This will give you an understanding of the information in your matter, and can be used to determine the starting point of your investigation.

More information on setting up Insights can be found in the topic Insights. If you do not have access to this functionality yet, please contact ZyLAB.

Insights used in Document Review

View the entities that are detected per Entity Type.

  • In Document View, on the right side of the screen, select Insights. 
     
  • To view all detected entities for the entity type Geographic Area, select it.
    For a specific area, select the arrow next to the Geographic Area icon and select the area you are looking for. Detected entites will be highlighted in orange.
    Only entities that are enabled in Entity Management are shown.
