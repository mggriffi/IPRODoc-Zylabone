Auto Redactions

In general, when conducting an internal investigation, you do not have to redact personal information in your report. In case you do have to do this, it is possible to redact or pseudonymize information. Automatically locate and redact keywords, phrases, and personal information (names, social security numbers, credit card numbers, email addresses, and more). Create Auto Redaction Rules to automatically redact the information. Auto Redaction Rules can be copied and pasted into additional matters.
