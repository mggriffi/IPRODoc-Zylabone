Produce Documents

No redaction is necessary for the purpose of this process, as the aim is only to get an estimate of the breached data. It is also not necessary to produce all the documents, only a report of the types of data and people involved is required.

After the documents have been assessed for relevance and it is clear which personal data has been leaked through search and entity extraction, the data found must be produced in a document. This report is produced to the supervisory authority and the data subjects, if necessary.

To produce a report, view the documents in Document List View.


Click on the wheel icon on the right-hand side.


You can choose the DSAR view, or you can click on columns and choose the different columns you want in the report. Refer to the DSAR Use Case for instructions on how to create the report—Produce Report
