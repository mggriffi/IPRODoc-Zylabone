Search

The DSAR procedure involves a somewhat detailed searching process, as all the personal data belonging to the requester are supposed to be found. However, for the purposes of handling a personal data breach and notification thereof, the controller needs to have an estimate of the kinds of personal data and data subjects involved, and an estimate number hereof.

Once the data is uploaded into ZyLAB ONE, the user can first use the Facet view to get a quick overview of the numbers and types of data involved.

A thorough search can be performed for more precise information. However, as time is of the essence in such cases, it is important to be fast and efficient.
