Anonymizing

After tagging a document with the Personal data tag and deciding that it is of importance to store the document, but there is no applicable legal ground (Purpose) for storing the document, it first needs to be anonymized.

Anonymizing personal data means removing the option for the data subject to be attributed to personal data, within a document. This can be easily done in ZyLAB ONE by using one of the two available options: Redaction or Auto-redaction. The difference between the two is one is performed manually, and the other is performed automatically by ZyLAB ONE.
