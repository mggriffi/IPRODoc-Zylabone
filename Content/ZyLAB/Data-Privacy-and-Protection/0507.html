Redact

With the help of entity extraction and the Insights feature of ZyLAB ONE, users can easily extract various data from documents, such as: names, phone numbers, email addresses, locations, credit card numbers, etc.

 

Example

Enron, with the help of an IT specialist, has uploaded the leaked data into ZyLAB ONE. Entity extraction can be used to find out which email addresses have been leaked and which personal data may have been involved. This feature extracts all entities from documents.
